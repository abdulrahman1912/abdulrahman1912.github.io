    About Us

    Mental Haven was established in 1999. It was founded by Abdullahi Abdulrahman who has an underying passion of helping people. Mental Haven came to being with lots of people who had this same agenda as the founder and it became a home for people with mental health issues. Provision of time, and a space to duel in where you can be yourself and express your hurt, pain and feelings was why Mental Haven was created. We are all suffering everyday you deserve people who can share that burden with you.

    How It Works

    You can either book a visit via our contacts provided for a one on one meeting. A safe zone where you can pour out your worries. Our counselors are free to talk to. Just send us a mail via our email or call any of the hotlines.

    You can also speak to our counselors by sending them an email for an online session. Its a simple process. After sending a message to us via our email, we send you a feed-back message containing a zoom link which we make use of for our online session. We promise to be there for you.
